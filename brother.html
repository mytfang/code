On Surveillance

from the Anti-Surveillance Manifesto

Big Brother is watching, so hide your data. In the digital age, every thing we do, watch, and type can be monitored, and our data sold to the highest bidder. It is time we fight back against by creating situations where the cost of deciphering our information exceeds its market value.
